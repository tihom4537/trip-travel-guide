# Privacy Policy for Trip: Travel Guide

_Last updated: March 30, 2026_

---

## Overview

Trip: Travel Guide is a simple, read-only travel guide app that displays tourist places across India. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we do and do not collect.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

Trip: Travel Guide does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app has:

- No user accounts
- No login or registration
- No backend servers
- No transmission or storage of user data

---

## Information We Collect Automatically

We do not collect any usage data, analytics, or device information through the app itself. If you downloaded the app through a third-party platform such as the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, that platform may collect certain information as per their own privacy policies, which are independent of ours.

---

## Internet Access

The app may use an internet connection solely to load images or content related to tourist destinations. No personal data is sent or received during this process.

---

## Third-Party Services

Trip: Travel Guide does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics tools that could collect your data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages, including children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
